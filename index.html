About Us

Our Humble Beginnings
Established in 2015, Steven Endriga General Store was born out of a desire to offer quality clothing for hardworking Filipinos. Our journey began as a small shop in Barangay Poblacion, General Santos City—growing thanks to loyal customers and word-of-mouth.
What We Stand For:
  • Makabayan Fashion – Celebrating local craftsmanship and design
  • Affordability Without Compromise – Great style for all budgets
  • Customer-First Approach – Service with a smile
Mission: Empower every Filipino to express themselves through affordable, stylish clothing.
Vision: Become Mindanao’s leading fashion retail brand by honoring our roots and embracing modern fashion.
