Privacy Policy

At Steven Endriga General Store, we value your privacy and work diligently to safeguard your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data you share with us when shopping or interacting with our store.

What Information Do We Collect?

  • Basic details you provide, such as your name, email, address, and phone number.
  • Order details, including products purchased and delivery preferences.
  • Payment information, which is processed securely via trusted third-party services.
  • Any feedback or inquiries you share with our team.

How Do We Use Your Information?

  • To process your orders and deliver products to your specified address.
  • To communicate with you regarding your order, status updates, and customer service requests.
  • To personalize your shopping experience and offer relevant products or promotions.
  • To improve our store and services based on your feedback.

How Is Your Data Protected?

  • We use secure systems and protocols to store and manage your information.
  • Payment details are handled through encrypted third-party gateways; we never store your card or wallet info.
  • Only authorized team members with a need to know may access your information.

Sharing Your Information

  • Your personal data is never sold or traded to outside parties.
  • We may share relevant details with delivery partners or payment processors only to fulfill your order.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You may update or correct your personal information by contacting us at contact@stevenendriga-store.com.
  • If you wish to review, delete, or restrict your data, please reach out and we’ll assist promptly.

Updates to Our Policy

We may revise this privacy policy as needed to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here, and we encourage you to review the policy regularly.

Questions?

If you need clarification or have concerns about how your data is handled, please contact us at contact@stevenendriga-store.com or call +63 956 901 7382. We’re here to help!
